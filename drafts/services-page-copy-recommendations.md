OVERVIEW
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The services page is the primary conversion page. StoryBrand alignment
should focus on:
1. Leading with the problem (depletion/running on empty)
2. Addressing both sub-avatars (Preparer + Rebuilder)
3. Positioning Alicia as the Guide
4. Clear Plan (Uncover → Rebuild → Sustain)
5. Stakes (what happens without support) — "Healthier mamas make healthier homes"
6. Success (the transformation) — Energy to be present + create healthy home environment

KEY INSIGHT: These women are motivated by their symptoms, but also deeply
motivated by creating healthy environments for their kids to thrive in.
The Stakes and Success sections now address both motivations.

3. PROBLEM SECTION ("Sound Familiar?")
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CURRENT:
─────────────────────────────────
Generic problem cards about labs, exhaustion, not knowing where
to start, etc.

RECOMMENDED:
─────────────────────────────────
Title: "ARE YOU RUNNING ON EMPTY?"

Reframe as two columns for the two avatars:

[PREPARING FOR PREGNANCY]
□ Fatigue that doesn't resolve with rest
□ Feeling wired but tired—stress hits harder than it used to
□ Subtle cycle changes or inconsistent ovulation
□ Hair shedding, brittle nails, or muscle tension
□ You want to enter pregnancy with reserves—not already depleted

[REBUILDING AFTER BIRTH]
□ Exhaustion deeper than sleep deprivation
□ Brain fog, reduced focus, emotional flatness
□ Cycles slow to return or unpredictable
□ Prolonged hair loss months after birth
□ Wondering if this exhaustion is your new normal

"These aren't signs something is wrong with you. They may be
signals your body is asking for support it hasn't received yet."
