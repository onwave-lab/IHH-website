## VSL Script

**[ON CAMERA — Alicia, warm + direct]**

You're doing everything you've been told to do. Eating better, taking your prenatal, trying to get more sleep. But you're still dragging through the afternoon, your cravings won't quit, and you just... don't feel like yourself.

So you go to your doctor. They run labs. And they tell you everything looks normal.

**[VOICEOVER — b-roll of woman looking tired, scrolling phone, staring at lab results]**

Here's what I see over and over again working with women — "normal" labs, but a body that's clearly struggling. The fatigue, the mood swings, the hair changes, the brain fog — those aren't nothing. They're signals.

The problem is, standard blood work is designed to catch disease. It's not designed to show you whether your body actually has the reserves it needs — especially if you're planning for pregnancy or trying to rebuild after one.

**[ON CAMERA — Alicia]**

That's where a different approach may help.

I'm Alicia Harrison. I'm a Family Nurse Practitioner, and I take a foundations-first approach to wellness. I use tools like HTMA — Hair Tissue Mineral Analysis — alongside functional lab work to look at what's happening beneath the surface. Not just whether you're "in range," but whether your body is actually set up to thrive.

**[VOICEOVER — b-roll of calm workspace, HTMA sample, woman smiling]**

And it starts with a conversation. No pressure, no sales pitch — just an honest look at where you are and whether this approach makes sense for your goals.

**[ON CAMERA — Alicia, warm close]**

If you're a Kentucky resident and you've been feeling like something is off — even though everyone keeps telling you you're fine — I'd love to talk.

Book a free discovery call. The link is right below.

**[TEXT ON SCREEN:]** *Book Your Free Discovery Call → intentionholistichealth.com/apply*

## StoryBrand Breakdown

| Element | Script Application |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Character** | Women doing "everything right" but still not feeling well |
| **External Problem** | Fatigue, cravings, brain fog despite eating better |
| **Internal Problem** | Frustration — feeling like something is wrong but no answers |
| **Philosophical Problem** | Women deserve to understand what's actually going on in their bodies |
| **Guide (Empathy)** | "Here's what I see over and over again" |
| **Guide (Authority)** | Family Nurse Practitioner, functional approach, HTMA |
| **Plan** | Functional testing + foundations-first approach |
| **CTA** | Book a free discovery call |
| **Failure Avoided** | Continuing to guess, entering pregnancy depleted |
| **Success** | Body set up to thrive, clarity, confidence |

## Compliance Notes

- All health claims use qualifying language ("may help", "signals", "what I see")
- No use of "coach," "consult," or "mentor"
- Kentucky residency requirement stated on camera
- No definitive outcome promises
