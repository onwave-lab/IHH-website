  • Exhaustion deeper than sleep deprivation
  • Brain fog, reduced focus, emotional flatness

  • Prolonged hair loss months after birth
  • Wondering if this exhaustion is your new normal

These aren't signs something is wrong with you. They may be signals your body is asking for support it hasn't received yet.
